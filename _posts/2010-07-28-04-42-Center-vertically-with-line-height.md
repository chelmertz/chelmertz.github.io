title: "Center vertically with `line-height`"
permalink: "center-vertically-with-line-height"
tags: [css]
---

For an <code>a</code>-element:
<div class="CodeRay">
<div class="code">
<pre>display: block;
height: 100px;
line-height: 100px;
width: 200px;
text-align: center;</pre>
</div>
</div>
This will make the text center both vertically and horizontally!
